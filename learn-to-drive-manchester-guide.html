Irlam Local Area Guide

Lessons around Irlam use real local roads including Silver Street, Irlam Wharf Road and Astley Road, so by the time you're ready for your test you've already driven the streets you'll use every day after passing. The Liverpool and Manchester Railway's crossing of Chat Moss near Irlam, achieved by floating the track bed on a raft of branches and soil over the peat bog, was one of the great engineering triumphs of the 1820s and is still in use today.

We also plan around school-run traffic near Irlam Endowed Primary School and Irlam Primary School, using quieter spots like AJ Bell Stadium for early manoeuvre practice before stepping up to busier sections of Silver Street.

Test centre: most learners around Irlam test at Salford (Fire Station) Driving Test Centre, Liverpool Street, Salford, M5 4LE; mock tests are planned around the routes examiners actually use from there.
