What Is the PassFirst Guarantee?

Learning to drive is a significant investment. You pay for lessons, you prepare for months, and then everything comes down to a 40-minute test. At most driving schools, if you do not pass, the answer is simple: pay for more lessons at full price.

DriveSQ does it differently.
