What’s Included in Every Package

Every DriveSQ block booking comes with more than just driving hours. We provide a complete learning experience designed to get you test-ready as efficiently as possible, with support at every stage of your journey.

Structured Learning Plan
Every student receives a personalised learning plan built around their current ability and target test date. Your instructor maps out exactly which skills you will cover each week, from basic car control through to advanced manoeuvres and independent driving. This structured approach means no wasted lessons and measurable progress every session. You always know where you are in your journey and what comes next.
Regular Progress Reviews
At the end of every two to three lessons, your instructor provides a detailed progress review. We discuss what you have mastered, identify areas that need more work, and adjust your learning plan accordingly. This keeps you motivated and ensures you are always working on the skills that matter most for your test. Many students tell us this structured feedback is what sets DriveSQ apart from other schools.
Mock Test Included
Every block booking of 10 hours or more includes a free mock driving test conducted under realistic test conditions. Your instructor takes you through the same routes used by DVSA examiners at your local test centre, marking faults exactly as an examiner would. This gives you a clear picture of your test readiness and dramatically reduces nerves on the actual day. Students who complete a mock test pass at a significantly higher rate.
Test-Day Car Provided
On the day of your practical driving test, we provide the same dual-control car you have been learning in at no extra charge. You drive a vehicle you know and trust, with familiar controls, mirrors, and dimensions. Your instructor will also take you for a warm-up drive before the test to settle your nerves and get you into the right mindset. There is no additional fee for test-day use of the car.
Priority Booking
Block booking students receive priority access to our most popular time slots. While pay-as-you-go students may need to wait for availability, block booking students get first choice of morning, afternoon, or evening sessions. This means you can lock in a consistent weekly schedule that fits around your work, college, or university commitments without worrying about losing your preferred slot.
Flexible Scheduling
Life happens, and we understand that. Block booking students can reschedule any lesson with 48 hours notice at no charge. You can also adjust your session lengths — switch between one-hour and two-hour lessons depending on your energy and availability that week. If you need to pause your lessons for a holiday or exam period, your remaining hours are held for you with no expiry date and no penalty.
Advantages

Why Block Book Your Lessons?

Consistency Builds Confidence

The single biggest factor in how quickly a learner driver becomes test-ready is consistency. Students who take regular, weekly lessons progress significantly faster than those who book sporadically with gaps of two or three weeks between sessions. When you block book with DriveSQ, you commit to a regular schedule that keeps your skills sharp and your muscle memory developing. You do not lose momentum between lessons, and you do not waste time at the start of each session re-learning what you covered last time. Block booking creates the discipline and rhythm that turns nervous beginners into confident, test-ready drivers in the shortest possible time.

The Consistency Advantage

Students who take two lessons per week consistently pass their test in an average of 12 weeks. Students who take one lesson per week with occasional gaps typically take 6–9 months to reach the same standard. Block booking locks in your schedule and keeps you on track.

Structured Progression, Not Random Practice

Without a structured plan, driving lessons can feel like random practice sessions where you cover whatever comes to mind on the day. Block booking changes that entirely. When you commit to a package, your DriveSQ instructor builds a personalised syllabus that takes you from your current ability level through every skill you need for the test, in the right order. Early lessons focus on car control, clutch work, and basic road positioning. Middle lessons introduce junctions, roundabouts, and dual carriageways. Later lessons cover manoeuvres, independent driving, and mock tests. Every hour has a purpose, and every session builds directly on the one before it. This is how professional driving instruction should work — and it is exactly what you get when you block book.

Build a Strong Instructor Relationship

Learning to drive is a deeply personal experience. You are sitting in a confined space with someone for hours at a time, making mistakes, feeling frustrated, and gradually building a skill that will last a lifetime. The relationship between student and instructor matters enormously. When you block book, you are guaranteed the same instructor for every lesson. They learn your strengths, understand your weaknesses, know when to push you and when to back off. They remember what you struggled with last week and can pick up exactly where you left off. This continuity is impossible when you book individual lessons with whoever happens to be available. At DriveSQ, your instructor becomes your coach, your mentor, and your biggest advocate on test day.

Save Time and Money

The DVSA recommends approximately 47 hours of professional tuition for the average learner. Students who block book and follow a structured plan typically need 15–20% fewer hours than those who book ad hoc, because less time is wasted on revision and catching up. That means fewer lessons, lower total cost, and a faster route to your driving licence.

Lock in Your Preferred Time Slots

Manchester is a busy city with thousands of learner drivers competing for lesson slots, especially during peak times like after school, after work, and on weekends. When you pay as you go, you are always at risk of losing your preferred time to someone who books before you. Block booking removes that uncertainty entirely. Your weekly slots are reserved in advance for the duration of your package. If you prefer Tuesday evenings and Saturday mornings, those slots are yours. No scrambling to book, no settling for inconvenient times, and no gaps in your schedule because your preferred slot was taken.

Best Value — 40-Hour Package

Our 40-Hour Complete package at £1,200 works out to just £30 per hour — saving you £120 compared to booking 40 individual lessons at the standard rate. For complete beginners, this is the most cost-effective way to go from zero experience to a full driving licence.
