Mastering Wilmslow Road

Road Overview

About Wilmslow Road

Wilmslow Road runs through south Manchester's busiest student and residential areas. The Curry Mile section in Rusholme is particularly challenging with double-parked cars, delivery vehicles, and constant pedestrian activity.

This road passes through: Rusholme, Fallowfield, Withington, Didsbury, East Didsbury.

Hazards on Wilmslow Road

Double-parked cars on Curry Mile
Students crossing without looking
Cycle traffic sharing the road
Bus stops with vehicles pulling out
Narrow sections in Didsbury village
Speed bumps approaching schools

MSPSL on Wilmslow Road

Constant mirror checks needed as cyclists weave through traffic. Signal early when overtaking parked cars. Position to the crown of the road when passing parked vehicles. Speed must be low through the Curry Mile — 20mph feels about right.

Common Mistakes

Driving too fast through the Curry Mile
Not checking for cyclists before opening doors
Missing pedestrians between parked cars
Getting stuck behind buses and panicking
Not adjusting speed for school zones
DriveSQ Student Tip

Practice this road at different times — it's completely different at 10am versus 10pm. Curry Mile at night has different hazards (restaurants, deliveries) than during the day.
