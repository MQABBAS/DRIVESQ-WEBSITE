Pull Up on the Right in Denton

Pull up on the right side of the road, reverse two car lengths, then rejoin traffic.

Step-by-Step Pull Up on the Right

1
Check mirrors and signal right
2
Cross to the right side and stop close to the kerb
3
Reverse straight for two car lengths, checking all around
4
Signal left, check mirrors and blind spot, rejoin traffic
