Parents’ Guide: Supervising Practice in Manchester

Support your learner with safe, structured extra miles around Manchester. This guide keeps home practice aligned with their lessons.

Simple Framework

  • Agree a goal before each drive (mirrors, roundabouts, parking)
  • Pick routes that match that goal (quiet → busier)
  • Debrief: 3 wins, 1 focus, next steps
  • Share your log with your DriveSQ instructor
