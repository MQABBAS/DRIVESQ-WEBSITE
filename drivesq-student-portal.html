Portal Overview

One Portal. Every Tool You Need.

The Student Portal brings together theory preparation, test simulation, and learning analytics into a single platform designed specifically for DriveSQ learners across Greater Manchester.

Question Bank
Over 700 DVSA-standard questions organised across all 14 official categories. Each question includes a detailed explanation to deepen your understanding of road rules and safe driving practices.
Timed Mock Tests
Sit 14 unique full-length mock exams that replicate the real DVSA theory test. Fifty questions, strict time limits, and instant results tell you exactly whether you are ready to book your test.
Hazard Perception
Interactive hazard perception clips train you to spot developing hazards on the road. Build the quick-response instincts that examiners look for, so you walk in confident on test day.
Progress Tracking
Visual dashboards show your scores over time, highlight weak categories, and confirm when you are consistently hitting pass-mark territory. Data-driven revision beats guesswork every time.
Theory Preparation

Master Every Theory Category

The DVSA theory test draws from 14 categories, and our portal covers all of them in depth. Rather than memorising answers, you will understand the reasoning behind each rule — which is exactly what the DVSA wants to see.

  • Alertness — Observation and anticipation on the road
  • Attitude — Consideration for other road users
  • Safety & Your Vehicle — Vehicle maintenance and checks
  • Safety Margins — Stopping distances and weather effects
  • Hazard Awareness — Identifying and responding to dangers
  • Vulnerable Road Users — Cyclists, pedestrians, motorcyclists
  • Road Signs & Markings — Regulatory and advisory signs

Plus seven more categories covering motorway rules, rules of the road, vehicle handling, documents, incidents, vehicle loading, and environmental matters. Every area is represented in our 700+ question bank.

Smart Study Mode

The portal remembers which questions you get wrong and resurfaces them more frequently. This spaced repetition approach means you spend your revision time on topics that actually need work, not ones you have already nailed.

Detailed Explanations

Every single question includes a thorough explanation of the correct answer. You will learn the "why" behind each road rule, making it easier to apply your knowledge to real driving situations — not just the test.

Exam Readiness Indicator

The portal analyses your mock test scores and category performance to give you a clear readiness rating. You will know exactly when you are prepared to book your DVSA theory test with confidence.
