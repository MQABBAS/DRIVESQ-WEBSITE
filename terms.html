2⚡ DriveSQ PassFirst Package — Terms
What is PassFirst? The ⚡ DriveSQ PassFirst package (£1,100) includes a full structured driving programme. If you do not pass your practical driving test on your first attempt after completing the package, DriveSQ will provide FREE support lessons to prepare you for your next test attempt.

To qualify for the PassFirst guarantee, all of the following conditions must be met:

  • You must have purchased the full ⚡ DriveSQ PassFirst package at the stated price of £1,100.
  • You must follow all instructor guidance throughout your lessons, including recommended focus areas and driving techniques.
  • You must complete all mock tests recommended by your instructor prior to your practical test. Declining or skipping recommended mock tests will void the guarantee.
  • You must attend all scheduled lessons without excessive cancellations (see Section 3). More than 3 late cancellations or no-shows may void the guarantee at DriveSQ's discretion.
  • You must not have refused lesson content or specific manoeuvres that your instructor deemed necessary for test readiness.
  • Your practical test must have been taken at a DVSA test centre within Greater Manchester that your instructor approved as suitable.

Scope of FREE support lessons:

  • Free support lessons are limited to preparing for one additional test attempt only.
  • The number of free support lessons provided is at the sole discretion of your DriveSQ instructor based on your assessed needs.
  • Free support lessons are subject to instructor availability and cannot be guaranteed within a specific timeframe.
  • Free support lessons do not include the cost of your second practical test booking — this remains the learner's responsibility.
  • Free lessons are non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash or credit.
The ⚡ PassFirst guarantee is a Summer 2026 limited promotion only. DriveSQ reserves the right to withdraw or modify this offer at any time for new bookings. Existing bookings will honour the terms at the time of purchase.

DriveSQ aims to be fair and transparent with all refund requests. The following policy applies to all lesson packages and block bookings.

Important: If you request a refund for a block or package booking, DriveSQ will calculate the value of lessons already completed at the standard rate of £35 per hour. This amount will be deducted from your refund.

How refunds are calculated:

Scenario Refund Amount

6Lesson Conduct & Safety

The safety of our learners, instructors, and the public is our absolute priority. The following rules apply during all DriveSQ lessons:

  • You must arrive for every lesson in a fit and legal state to drive — sober, rested, and not under the influence of any substance.
  • Your instructor has the right to terminate a lesson immediately if they believe it is unsafe to continue. A full lesson fee will be charged in this event.
  • You must bring your valid UK provisional or full driving licence to every lesson. DriveSQ cannot lawfully allow you to drive without sight of your licence.
  • Mobile phones must be stowed away and not used during lessons. Hands-free use is not permitted while in control of the vehicle.
  • Verbal or physical abuse towards instructors will result in immediate termination of all bookings with no refund of unused hours.
  • Any damage to the DriveSQ vehicle caused by reckless or deliberate conduct may be charged to the learner. Normal driving errors during lessons are not charged.
  • You must wear a seatbelt at all times during lessons.
  • If you hold a full licence and are taking refresher lessons, you remain legally responsible for your driving at all times.
7Learner Eligibility
  • You must hold a valid UK provisional driving licence before your first lesson.
  • You must be at least 17 years of age to take driving lessons in the UK.
  • You must meet the legal eyesight requirements — you must be able to read a standard number plate from 20 metres. If you need glasses or contact lenses to do so, you must wear them for every lesson and for your test.
  • You must declare any medical conditions or DVLA notifications that may affect your ability to drive. DriveSQ is not liable for any consequences arising from undisclosed medical conditions.
  • Non-UK residents must hold a valid provisional licence entitlement valid in Great Britain.
