How It Works for Salford Students

Getting your driving licence while studying at the University of Salford is simpler than you think. Whether you are a first-year in Peel Park Quarter or a final-year in a Langworthy house share, DriveSQ fits around your university life.

Step 1: Message Us on WhatsApp

Send a quick message to 07352 932003 on WhatsApp. Tell us whether you want manual or automatic, your experience level, and your general availability around lectures. We will match you with a DVSA-approved instructor who covers your area.

Step 2: We Come to You

Your instructor picks you up from campus, your accommodation or any convenient location in the M5 area. No need to trek across Salford to reach a driving school office. Lessons are available mornings, afternoons, evenings and weekends.

Step 3: Learn on Real Test Routes

From your very first lesson, you will drive on the roads that matter — The Crescent, Frederick Road, Regent Road and the surrounding residential streets. As you progress, we introduce dual carriageways, roundabouts and the specific manoeuvres tested at local test centres.

Access our Student Portal with 700+ official DVSA theory questions, 14 full mock tests and hazard perception clips. It works offline as a PWA, so you can revise on the tram, in the library or between lectures with no data needed.

Step 5: Pass First Time

With our 90%+ first-time pass rate and the PassFirst guarantee, you are in the strongest position to pass. And if you do not pass first time, we provide free support lessons until you do.
