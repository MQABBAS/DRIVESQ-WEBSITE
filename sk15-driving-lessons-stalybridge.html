Before altering course anywhere in SK15, the sequence is always: interior mirror, relevant door mirror, signal, adjust position, regulate speed, then look. On Stamford Street, traffic closes gaps fast — skipping any step invites danger. We drill this until it becomes reflex.

Reading the Road

Developing hazards in Stalybridge include steep hills everywhere (Peak District edge) and vehicles pulling out from side roads on Stamford Street. DriveSQ trains you to scan 12 seconds ahead — roughly the length of three lamp posts at 30mph — giving you maximum reaction time.
