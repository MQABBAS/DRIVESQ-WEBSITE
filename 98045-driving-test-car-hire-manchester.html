Driving Test Car Hire in New Islington

Test Day Vehicle Hire

If you have learned privately, with a different instructor, or need a dual-control vehicle for your test, DriveSQ offers test-day car hire across Manchester. You get a modern, well-maintained, dual-control vehicle that meets all DVSA requirements, plus a warm-up drive before your test to familiarise yourself with the car.

What Is Included

  • Modern dual-control vehicle (manual or automatic available)
  • 60-minute warm-up lesson before your test time
  • Pick-up from your home or a convenient location
  • Drive to and from the test centre
  • Vehicle adjusted for your driving position and mirror preferences
  • Insurance for test purposes included

Important Information

We strongly recommend booking at least one familiarisation lesson before your test day — even experienced drivers need time to adjust to a new vehicle's clutch, brakes, and steering feel. The car will be fully road-legal, clean, and in excellent mechanical condition. We cover all Greater Manchester test centres: Cheetham Hill, West Didsbury, Sale, Stockport, Hyde, and Bredbury.

Pricing

Test-day car hire including the 60-minute warm-up is £70. An additional familiarisation lesson (recommended) is charged at the standard hourly rate. Book at least one week in advance to guarantee availability on your test date.
