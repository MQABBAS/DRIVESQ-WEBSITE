Benefits of Learning Automatic With DriveSQ

Automatic cars are the future of driving in the UK. Here is why more Manchester learners are making the switch.

100% Focus on the Road

Without the distraction of clutch control, gear changes, and hill starts, you can devote all your attention to hazard awareness, positioning, and mirror checks — the skills that actually matter on test day.

Faster to Test Standard

Most automatic learners reach test readiness 8–12 hours sooner than manual learners. That is a significant saving in both time and money, getting you on the road and driving independently weeks earlier.

No Price Premium

DriveSQ charges £35/hr for automatic lessons — exactly the same as manual. Most Manchester schools add £3–£8 per hour for automatic. Over 30 hours, that surcharge adds up to £90–£240 extra elsewhere.

Ideal for Anxious Learners

Stalling at junctions and rolling back on hills are two of the biggest anxiety triggers for new drivers. Automatic transmission eliminates both entirely, letting nervous learners build confidence from lesson one.

Future-Proof Your Licence

Electric vehicles are all automatic. By 2030, most new cars sold in the UK will be electric. Learning automatic now means you are already prepared for the vehicles you will be driving for decades to come.

The Automatic vs Manual Debate — Settled

One common concern is that an automatic licence restricts you to only driving automatic vehicles. While this is technically true, consider the reality: over 70% of new cars registered in the UK are now automatic or electric. This figure increases every year. Major manufacturers are phasing out manual gearboxes entirely. By the time most learners today have been driving for a few years, finding a manual car will be the exception rather than the rule.

For learners who want their licence as quickly and stress-free as possible, automatic is objectively the faster route. You spend zero hours learning clutch bite points, zero hours practising gear changes, and zero time stalling at busy roundabouts. All that time goes directly into becoming a safer, more confident road user.
