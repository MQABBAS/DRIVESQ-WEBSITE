Common Questions

FAQs for UoM Students

Can you pick me up from my halls in Fallowfield?
Absolutely. We pick up from Owens Park, Oak House, Woolton Hall, Richmond Park, and all private halls along Wilmslow Road and Moseley Road. Just share your location on WhatsApp and we’ll be there.
Can I book lessons between lectures?
Yes — many UoM students book 1 or 2 hour lessons during gaps in their timetable. We can pick up from the Oxford Road campus and drop you back afterwards. Perfect for making use of those free Wednesday afternoons.
What’s the PassFirst guarantee?
If you don’t pass your practical driving test on the first attempt, we provide FREE additional support lessons until you pass. It’s our promise that we won’t leave you stranded after a setback.
Do you offer intensive courses during uni holidays?
Yes! Many UoM students take advantage of Christmas, Easter, and summer breaks for intensive driving courses. Complete your learning in 1–2 weeks while you have time off from studies.
Is the theory app really free?
Yes, our Student Portal is completely free for all DriveSQ students. It includes 700+ DVSA theory questions, 14 mock tests, hazard perception training, and progress tracking. It even works offline as a PWA.
I’m an international student — can I learn with you?
Of course! We teach many international students at UoM. If you hold a valid provisional UK driving licence, you can start lessons straight away. We’re patient, experienced, and used to teaching complete beginners.
