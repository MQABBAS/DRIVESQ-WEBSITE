Intensive Driving Courses in Manchester: Costs & What to Expect

If you need your licence quickly — perhaps for a new job, university, or simply because you're eager to get on the road — an intensive driving course might be the perfect option.

Intensive courses (sometimes called "crash courses") compress your learning into one or two weeks of daily lessons. Instead of learning over several months with weekly one-hour sessions, you'll have extended daily sessions that accelerate your progress.

How Much Do Intensive Courses Cost in Manchester?

Across Manchester, intensive courses typically range from £950 to £1,500 depending on the number of hours and the school. DriveSQ offers a 30-hour intensive package for £990, which includes daily 3–5 hour sessions with your dedicated DVSA approved instructor.

Is an Intensive Course Right for You?

Intensive courses work best for learners who:

  • Already have their theory test passed
  • Can dedicate a full week or two to learning
  • Have some prior driving experience (even unofficial)
  • Need their licence for a specific deadline
