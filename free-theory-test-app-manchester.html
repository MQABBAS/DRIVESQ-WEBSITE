Complete Guide

How to Use the DriveSQ Free Theory Test App

Getting Started Is Simple

Open the app from any browser — there is nothing to download from the App Store or Google Play. Visit our theory app page, and you are ready to start practising. You can install it to your home screen as a Progressive Web App for instant access, just like a native app.

Practice by Category or Take Full Mock Tests

The app lets you choose how you want to study. Work through questions by category — covering topics like road signs, vehicle handling, safety margins, rules of the road, and more — or jump straight into a full 50-question timed mock test to simulate the real experience.

  • Category Practice: Focus on specific topics to build knowledge
  • Mock Tests: 14 full-length exams with realistic timing
  • Review Mode: Go back over questions you got wrong
  • Hazard Perception: Interactive training clips

Track Your Progress Over Time

Every session is tracked. You can see your overall score, category-by-category breakdown, and which questions you consistently get wrong. This makes your revision targeted and efficient — no wasting time on topics you have already mastered.

Pro Tip

Aim to consistently score 46/50 or above on mock tests before booking your theory test. The pass mark is 43/50, but aiming higher gives you a comfortable margin on test day.
