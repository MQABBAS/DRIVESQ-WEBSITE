Intensive Driving Courses in Wigan

Need your licence fast? DriveSQ intensive courses compress months of learning into focused 1-2 week programmes. Daily lessons, structured progression, and a test date at the end. Same £35/hr rate — no premium for speed.

How Intensive Courses Work

A DriveSQ intensive course replaces months of weekly lessons with daily concentrated sessions. You drive for 4-6 hours per day (with breaks), covering the full DVSA syllabus in compressed time. Skills learned in the morning are reinforced in the afternoon and consolidated the following day — creating rapid neural pathway development that weekly lessons cannot match.

Each day follows a structured plan: warm-up on familiar roads, introduction of new skills, practice in varied environments, and a progress review. By day five of a typical two-week course, most learners are driving independently on test-standard routes.

Course Options

1-Week Crash Course

30-36 hours over 5-6 days. Suited to learners with some prior experience or those who have already passed their theory test. Daily sessions of 5-6 hours with structured breaks. Test booked for day 6 or 7.

2-Week Standard Intensive

40-50 hours over 10-12 days. Ideal for complete beginners. Gentler pace with 4-5 hour daily sessions. Includes 2-3 mock tests. Test booked for the final day of week two.

Semi-Intensive

3-4 lessons per week for 3-4 weeks. Balances intensive learning with processing time. Suits people who cannot take full weeks off work. 2-3 hour sessions, 3-4 days per week.

What a Typical Intensive Day Looks Like

09:00-10:30: Warm-up drive on familiar roads. Review yesterday's progress. Introduce today's first new skill.

10:30-10:45: Break. Get out of the car, stretch, hydrate.

10:45-12:15: Practice new skill in varied locations. Combine with previously learned skills.

12:15-13:00: Lunch break. Complete rest from driving.

13:00-14:30: Introduce second new skill or practise test routes. Manoeuvre practice.

14:30-14:45: Break.

14:45-16:00: Extended independent driving practice. Route following. Mock test elements. Day review.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I pass with an intensive course?
With DriveSQ, complete beginners can reach test standard in 1-2 weeks with daily 4-6 hour sessions. Semi-experienced learners may be ready in 5-7 days. Your DriveSQ instructor assesses your starting level and provides an honest timeline.
Do intensive courses include a test date?
DriveSQ intensive packages include assistance booking your test date. We coordinate your lesson schedule to ensure you are test-ready on the booked date. If cancellation slots are available, we can sometimes arrange tests within days.
Are intensive courses more expensive?
DriveSQ charges the same £35/hr rate for intensive courses as weekly lessons. A 30-hour intensive course costs £1,050 (or £990 with block discount). There is no intensive surcharge.
Is it better to learn intensively or weekly?
Both approaches work. Intensive suits people with time off work, those needing a licence urgently, or learners who lose momentum between weekly sessions. Weekly suits people who need time to process skills between lessons.
What if I am not ready by my test date?
DriveSQ will be honest if you are not ready. We can reschedule your test and add additional intensive days. Our PassFirst guarantee means free support lessons if you fail — whether on intensive or standard courses.
