What You'll Cover in Your Weekend Lessons

Here is a typical progression for a learner taking weekly weekend lessons with DriveSQ. Every plan is personalised to your current ability and goals.

Weekends 1-2

Getting Started & Building Confidence

Your instructor assesses your starting point and introduces you to the fundamentals. You will cover cockpit drill, moving off and stopping, steering control, and basic gear changes. Sunday quiet roads are ideal for these first sessions.

Weekends 3-5

Junctions, Roundabouts & Road Awareness

Build on the basics with T-junctions, crossroads, mini roundabouts, and multi-lane roundabouts. You will develop your observation skills, learn to read road markings, and practise safe positioning in varied traffic conditions across Manchester.

Weekends 6-8

Manoeuvres & Dual Carriageways

Master all four test manoeuvres: parallel parking, forward and reverse bay parking, and pulling up on the right. You will also tackle dual carriageway driving, building confidence with higher speeds, merging, and lane discipline.

Weekends 9-11

Independent Driving & Test Routes

Practise navigating independently using the sat-nav and road signs. Your instructor will take you on popular routes from your nearest test centre so you are familiar with the roads, hazards, and junctions you are likely to encounter on test day.

Weekends 12+

Mock Tests & Exam Readiness

Complete full mock tests under realistic exam conditions. Your instructor provides detailed feedback on every aspect of your driving, helping you eliminate minor faults and walk into your practical test feeling calm, prepared, and confident.
