What We Practise

Simple, safe routines that turn fast-moving traffic into calm, predictable decisions.

  • Joining at speed: mirrors, signal, position, speed, look (MSPSL) plus effective acceleration lanes.
  • Safe overtaking: observation, timing, legal limits and smooth lane returns.
  • Lane discipline: keeping left, reading flow, avoiding “middle-lane drift”.
  • Exits & signage: planning early, correct signals and safe deceleration.
  • Night & wet weather: stopping distances, visibility and tyre grip awareness.
