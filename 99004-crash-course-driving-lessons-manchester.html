Can I really learn to drive in a week?
If you have some previous experience, yes. Complete beginners usually need 2-4 weeks. Your instructor will give an honest assessment on day one — we never push you to test before you are ready.
How many hours per day?
2-3 hours per session. We never do sessions longer than 3 hours because fatigue reduces learning quality. Most crash courses schedule one session per day, 5-6 days per week.
What if I need more hours than my package?
Additional hours are charged at the standard hourly rate. There is no pressure — if you need more time, we add it. If you need fewer hours than expected, we can adjust.
