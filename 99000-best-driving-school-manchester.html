How Our System Gets You Passed Faster

Phase 1: Foundation (Lessons 1-8)

Vehicle control, basic junctions, and traffic light navigation. We start on quiet roads near your home and build confidence with the pedals, steering, and mirrors before adding complexity. By lesson 8, you are driving in normal traffic with increasing independence.

Phase 2: Development (Lessons 9-20)

Roundabouts, dual carriageways, complex junctions, and lane discipline. We introduce Manchester's challenging roads — Princess Parkway, the A56, Palatine Road — and build your ability to read traffic, plan ahead, and maintain safe positioning. Manoeuvres (parallel parking, bay parking, pull up on right) are introduced and refined.

Phase 3: Test Readiness (Lessons 20-35)

Independent driving with sat-nav, examiner-route practice, mock tests, and fault elimination. We drive the actual routes examiners use at your chosen test centre. Mock tests identify any remaining weaknesses. Show-me-tell-me questions are rehearsed. By the end of this phase, you should be consistently passing mock tests with fewer than 5 minor faults.

Phase 4: Test Day

A 60-minute warm-up drive with your instructor, a final show-me-tell-me review, and calm delivery to the test centre door. Your instructor waits outside during the test and is there when you come out — hopefully with a pass certificate.
