Driving Test Mock Lessons Manchester

Get test-ready with confidence. At DriveSQ, our mock lessons recreate the exact rhythm and pressure of a real DVSA driving test in Manchester, so nothing on the day feels unfamiliar. You’ll experience a full examiner-style briefing, a realistic route with local complexities, and clear, score-linked feedback at the end. With structured practice and a supportive coaching style, you’ll convert good lessons into a polished performance when it matters most.

About Our Mock Lessons

Every mock is built around the DVSA marking criteria and your recent lesson history. We start with objectives, then run a full mock under quiet, examiner-like guidance. You’ll practise independent driving (following signs or sat-nav), manoeuvres such as bay or parallel parking, and controlled reversing with all-round observation. After the drive, we break down strengths, minor faults and any serious risks using plain language. You’ll leave with a written action plan that targets the biggest wins first, so your next practice session is focused and purposeful.

Top Tips for Driving Test Success

  • Practise on local routes and note busy times, lane markings and bus lanes
  • Use your action plan to prioritise two or three improvements per session
  • Slow the approach, widen the view: observations before every decision
  • Sleep well, hydrate and arrive early—routine builds calm confidence
