Rush Hour Skills You Need

1
Stop-Start Traffic Control
Master smooth clutch control for constant stopping and starting on Manchester Road. Avoid rolling back on inclines and keep a safe following distance.
2
Lane Discipline
In heavy traffic, changing lanes requires decisive mirror checks and confident signalling. DriveSQ teaches you to plan ahead so you are in the correct lane early.
3
Junction Blocking Awareness
Never enter a junction or box junction unless your exit is clear. This is a common test fail — and a common rush hour mistake on Mossley roads.
4
Staying Calm Under Pressure
Other drivers may honk or tailgate. Your DriveSQ instructor teaches you to stay focused on your driving, ignore pressure, and make safe decisions.
