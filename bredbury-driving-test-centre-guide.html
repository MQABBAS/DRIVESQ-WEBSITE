Bredbury Driving Test Centre — Complete Guide

Bredbury is Stockport's primary test centre. Your test literally begins on Stockport Road — and how you handle that first exit sets the tone for everything that follows. DriveSQ knows every route, every junction, every fail point.

Centre Profile

Bredbury Test Centre serves the entire Stockport borough and surrounding areas. Pass rate: approximately 44-48%. Routes traverse a mix of urban Stockport roads, village streets in Romiley and Woodley, and industrial sections around George Lane.

Route Challenges

The Test Centre Exit

Uniquely, your very first assessed manoeuvre at Bredbury is a right turn onto busy Stockport Road. This is not a gentle start — traffic flows constantly in both directions, and gap assessment under pressure is immediate. DriveSQ practises this specific exit until your confidence makes it feel routine.

George Lane Industrial Traffic

George Lane carries HGV traffic to and from industrial estates. Large vehicles restrict your view at junctions, require wider passing clearance, and create air turbulence when overtaking. DriveSQ teaches specific techniques for sharing roads with heavy goods vehicles safely.

Romiley Village Streets

Romiley village features narrow streets with parked cars on both sides, creating constant meeting situations. The examiner assesses your ability to identify who has priority, find safe passing places, and execute smooth give-and-take manoeuvres. DriveSQ practises these streets until meeting oncoming vehicles feels natural rather than stressful.

A560 Ashton Road Roundabouts

The A560 features several roundabouts that test lane discipline and observation. Approach speed, lane selection, and exit signalling must all be correct. DriveSQ breaks each roundabout into a step-by-step approach practised from every entry point.
