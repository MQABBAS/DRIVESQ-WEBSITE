Overview

Greater Manchester has seven practical driving test centres. Each has its own character, routes, and quirks. DriveSQ instructors teach at all of them — here is our insider guide.

Cheetham Hill Test Centre

Address: Crescent Business Park, Willenhall Road, M8 8LW
Routes include: Cheetham Hill Road, Queens Road, Crumpsall Lane, Bury Old Road, Waterloo Road
Challenges: Very busy Cheetham Hill Road with double-parked vehicles, complex light-controlled junctions, pedestrians
DriveSQ tip: Most routes head north through Crumpsall or east through Cheetham Hill. Practise the right turn from the test centre exit — it is the first thing the examiner sees.

West Didsbury Test Centre

Address: Cavendish Road, M20 1JG
Routes include: Palatine Road, Barlow Moor Road, Burton Road, Nell Lane, Wilmslow Road
Challenges: Residential streets with parked cars, school traffic, mini-roundabouts
DriveSQ tip: The Palatine Road / Barlow Moor Road roundabout features on almost every route. Practise your lane choice here until it is automatic.

Sale Test Centre

Address: Harboro Road, M33 5AJ
Routes include: Washway Road, Marsland Road, Brooklands Road, Northenden Road
Challenges: Washway Road rush-hour traffic, tram crossings, Sale town-centre one-way
DriveSQ tip: Sale routes often include the Dane Road tram crossing — always look both ways for trams, even if the road looks clear.

Bredbury Test Centre

Address: Stockport Road, SK6 2DH
Routes include: George Lane, Ashton Road, Lower Bents Lane, Stockport Road
Challenges: Busy exit from test centre onto Stockport Road, nearby industrial traffic, complex roundabouts
DriveSQ tip: The right-turn exit from the car park onto Stockport Road is your first test. Practise it at different times of day.

Failsworth Test Centre

Address: Ashton Road West, M35 0AD
Routes include: Ashton Road, Oldham Road, Broadway, Manchester Road
Challenges: Fast-moving A62 traffic, residential streets in Failsworth and Droylsden
DriveSQ tip: Routes often include the Broadway / Ashton Road roundabout — multi-lane and busy. Approach with a clear plan.

Hyde Test Centre

Address: Mottram Road, SK14 2NN
Routes include: Mottram Road, Market Street, Stockport Road, Manchester Road
Challenges: Steep hills, narrow town-centre streets, busy market area
DriveSQ tip: Mottram Road has significant gradients. Hill starts on a slope are almost guaranteed on test day — practise until they are effortless.

Spinningfields Local Area Guide

Lessons around Spinningfields use real local roads including Bridge Street, Deansgate and Hardman Street, so by the time you're ready for your test you've already driven the streets you'll use every day after passing. Spinningfields takes its name from a narrow Victorian street, Spinningfield, where cotton was historically prepared for weaving before the area was redeveloped into Manchester's modern financial district from the 1990s onward.

We also plan around school-run traffic near St Mary's RC Primary School (Hulme) and Webster Primary School (Denmark Road, Greenheys), using quieter spots like Manchester Crown Court (Crown Square) for early manoeuvre practice before stepping up to busier sections of Bridge Street.

Test centre: most learners around Spinningfields test at Cheetham Hill (Manchester) Test Centre, Alderglen Road, Cheetham, Manchester, M8 0AL; mock tests are planned around the routes examiners actually use from there.
