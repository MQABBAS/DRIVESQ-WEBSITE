Manual vs Automatic Driving Lessons in Manchester

One of the most common questions we receive is whether learners should take manual or automatic driving lessons in Manchester. At DriveSQ, we don't push you either way — and crucially, both cost the same: £33 per hour. There's no premium for automatic.

Manual driving lessons give you a full car licence that lets you drive any vehicle. Automatic lessons are increasingly popular for learners who find clutch control stressful, nervous learners who want to focus on road awareness first, and those commuting in busy Manchester traffic daily. Both are equally valid choices.
