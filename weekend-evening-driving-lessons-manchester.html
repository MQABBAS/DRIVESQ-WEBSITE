Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer Saturday and Sunday lessons?
Yes. DriveSQ operates seven days a week across Manchester. Saturday and Sunday lessons are available from 8am to 6pm at the standard £35/hr rate with no weekend surcharge.
How late can I have an evening lesson?
DriveSQ evening lessons run until approximately 8pm (later in summer when daylight extends). Evening sessions are excellent for experiencing night driving conditions during winter months.
Is there a surcharge for weekend or evening lessons?
No. DriveSQ charges £35/hr for every lesson regardless of day or time. No weekend premium, no evening supplement. The price is always the same.
Are roads quieter at weekends for learning?
Saturday mornings are often quieter than weekdays, making them excellent for nervous beginners. Sunday mornings are even quieter. Weekend afternoons carry moderate traffic. DriveSQ advises the best time slot for your current skill level.
