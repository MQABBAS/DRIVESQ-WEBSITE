Why Learn to Drive at Uni?

Manchester has over 100,000 university students across UoM, MMU, Salford, and Bolton. Learning to drive while at uni is smart: you have flexible hours, a permanent address for your provisional licence, and a driving licence is increasingly required for graduate jobs. Plus, Manchester's road network gives you experience that transfers anywhere in the UK.

Best Areas to Learn Near Manchester Unis


  • UoM / Fallowfield halls: Lessons start on quiet Fallowfield side streets, progress to Wilmslow Road and Palatine Road. Test at West Didsbury.

  • MMU (All Saints/Birley): Start in Hulme residential streets, progress to Chester Road and Princess Road. Test at Cheetham Hill or Sale.

  • Salford Uni: Start in Salford Crescent area, progress to Chapel Street and Eccles New Road. Test at Eccles.

  • Bolton Uni: Start in quiet Bolton residential streets, progress to Deane Road and the A666. Test at Bolton.
