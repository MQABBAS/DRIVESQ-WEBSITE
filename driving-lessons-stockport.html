Driving in Stockport — What to Expect

Stockport Test Centre Routes

The Stockport DVSA test centre sits on Wellington Road South, just south of the town centre near the A6/Travis Brow junction. Test routes typically head through Great Portwood Street, loop around the residential streets of Heaviley and Davenport, and return via the A626 towards the Dialstone Lane area. Our instructors have catalogued every turn, roundabout, and potential hazard on these routes so that nothing on test day catches you off guard.

Key Roads Around Stockport

Stockport’s road network provides outstanding variety for learner drivers. The A6 Wellington Road runs north-south through the borough, offering dual carriageway stretches with complex junctions at Hazel Grove and Heaviley. The A626 Marple Road climbs east through Offerton towards Marple Bridge, testing your hill-start ability. Closer to the centre, Dialstone Lane and Nangreave Road present residential challenges with parked cars and school zones. The A5145 links towards Cheadle, while M60 junctions 1–4 give post-pass motorway access for Pass Plus training.
