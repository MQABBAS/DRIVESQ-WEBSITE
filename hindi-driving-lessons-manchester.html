What Your Hindi Driving Lessons Cover

Every lesson is structured and tailored to your ability. Your Hindi speaking instructor will guide you through each stage in clear, fluent Hindi so nothing is lost in translation.

Stage 1

Vehicle Controls & Cockpit Drill (गाड़ी के नियंत्रण)

Your instructor begins by explaining all vehicle controls in Hindi — steering, mirrors, pedals, gears, and indicators. You will practise moving off and stopping safely in a quiet area until you feel comfortable with the basics of car handling.

Stage 2

Junctions, Roundabouts & Road Positioning (चौराहे और गोलचक्कर)

Progress to busier roads where your instructor teaches junction approaches, roundabout navigation, and correct lane positioning. All observations and mirror routines are explained in Hindi so you develop the right habits from the start.

Stage 3

Manoeuvres & Parking (पार्किंग और मैन्यूवर)

Master every test manoeuvre including parallel parking, bay parking (forward and reverse), and pulling up on the right. Your instructor breaks down each step in Hindi, making complex spatial awareness skills easy to grasp.

Stage 4

Dual Carriageways & Independent Driving (स्वतंत्र ड्राइविंग)

Build confidence on faster roads and practise independent driving using sat-nav and road signs. Your instructor explains speed management, merging, and lane discipline in Hindi while gradually introducing the English terms you will hear during the test.

Stage 5

Mock Tests & Exam Preparation (परीक्षा की तैयारी)

Complete full mock driving tests under real exam conditions. Your instructor provides detailed Hindi feedback after each mock so you know exactly what to improve. By this stage, you will feel fully prepared and confident for your practical test.
