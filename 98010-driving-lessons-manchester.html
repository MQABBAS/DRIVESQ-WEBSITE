Lesson Structure

Every DriveSQ lesson follows a clear structure: a brief recap of your last session, focused skill development, real-road practice, and a debrief with specific action points. Early lessons cover cockpit setup, moving off, stopping, and basic junctions. Intermediate lessons introduce roundabouts, dual carriageways, and complex junctions. Advanced lessons focus on test preparation, independent driving, and manoeuvres. Your instructor adapts the pace to your confidence level — no rushing, no holding back.

Pricing

Intensive course packages start from £300 for 10 hours. All prices include pick-up and drop-off, dual-control vehicle use, and lesson materials. We never charge extra for automatic lessons.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much are driving lessons in Manchester?

DriveSQ charges £28-£37 per hour depending on your area. Block bookings of 10 hours start from £300. Manual and automatic lessons are the same price.

How many driving lessons do I need?

The DVSA recommends an average of 45 hours of professional tuition. Many DriveSQ learners are test-ready in 30-40 hours thanks to our structured approach. Your instructor will give you a personalised estimate after your first lesson.

Do you pick up from home?

Yes. Your instructor picks you up from home, work, university, or any Manchester address at no extra charge.

Can I learn in an automatic car?

Yes, and we charge the same price for automatic and manual lessons. Many learners find automatic easier and faster to learn.
