Common Last-Minute Situations

Pre-Test Warm Up

The most popular last-minute booking is a pre-test warm-up lesson on the morning of a practical test. A 1–2 hour session driving the test routes immediately before your appointment refreshes your muscle memory, settles your nerves, and gets you into driving mode. Students who warm up consistently outperform those who arrive cold.

Unexpected Free Time

Work cancelled, lecture postponed, or plans fell through? Rather than losing the time, turn it into driving progress. A single unexpected lesson can cover an entire manoeuvre module or a complete mock test run, pushing your training forward by a week.

Test Date Brought Forward

DVSA cancellation slots can appear with just days’ notice. When your test is suddenly sooner than planned, last-minute intensive sessions help you consolidate skills and ensure you are genuinely ready. We can often fit in 2–4 extra hours within 24 to 48 hours of notification.
