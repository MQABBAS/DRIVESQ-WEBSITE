Why These Faults Happen

Most test faults aren't caused by lack of skill — they're caused by lack of familiarity with the specific roads. A learner who has never driven on Pagefield will naturally be less confident than someone who has practised it 20 times.

This is why DriveSQ focuses heavily on route-specific preparation. We don't just teach you to drive — we teach you to drive on the exact roads your examiner will take you.

Fault Categories
  • Observation: Not looking properly at junctions
  • Mirrors: Missing mirror checks before signalling
  • Positioning: Wrong lane or road position
  • Speed: Too fast or too slow for conditions
  • Signals: Late, missing, or misleading signals
