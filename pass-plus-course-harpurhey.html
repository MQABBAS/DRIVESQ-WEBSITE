Advanced Training

Pass Plus Modules

Pass Plus is a practical training course designed by the DVSA for newly qualified drivers. The standard driving test covers essential skills, but it cannot include motorway driving, extended night driving, or adverse weather conditions. Pass Plus fills these gaps.

New drivers are statistically the most at-risk group on UK roads. In Greater Manchester, 1 in 5 road casualties involves a driver under 25. Pass Plus significantly reduces this risk by building experience in controlled, instructor-guided conditions.

The 6 Pass Plus Modules

Module 1: Town Driving
Advanced town driving skills on Rochdale Road (A664) and surrounding Harpurhey streets. Complex junctions, bus lanes, cycle awareness, and pedestrian-heavy areas.
Module 2: All-Weather
Driving in rain, fog, ice, and strong winds. Manchester gets 150+ rain days per year — this module ensures you can handle every condition safely.
Module 3: Out of Town
Country roads, B-roads, and rural driving around Greater Manchester. Blind bends, narrow lanes, agricultural vehicles, and varying speed limits.
Module 4: Night Driving
Headlight use, dazzle recovery, judging speed and distance in darkness. Harpurhey residential streets and main roads after dark.
Module 5: Dual Carriageways
High-speed driving on the A34, A56, A6, and other Greater Manchester dual carriageways. Merging, overtaking, and maintaining safe following distances at speed.
Module 6: Motorways
M60, M62, M56, and M602 driving. Joining and leaving motorways, lane discipline, smart motorway rules, and emergency procedures.
