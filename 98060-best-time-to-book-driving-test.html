Best Time to Book Your Driving Test

Time of Day

Early morning tests (8:00-9:00 AM) mean less traffic on the roads, which can make the test feel easier. However, you may encounter school-run traffic. Mid-morning tests (10:00-11:00 AM) avoid both rush hour and school runs — many instructors consider this the ideal slot. Afternoon tests (1:00-3:00 PM) offer lighter traffic but school-run congestion returns from 3:00 PM. Avoid 4:00-5:00 PM tests if possible — rush hour traffic increases stress and the risk of traffic-related faults.

Day of Week

  • Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday — generally recommended. Traffic patterns are most predictable mid-week
  • Monday — can be busy with traffic after the weekend but is fine
  • Friday — slightly busier with weekend traffic building. Avoid Friday afternoon if possible
  • Saturday — tests are available at some centres. Less commuter traffic but more leisure traffic

Seasonal Considerations

  • Spring (March-May) — good weather, longer days, moderate traffic. Many instructors consider this ideal
  • Summer (June-August) — school holiday period means less school-run traffic but more general traffic and tourists
  • Autumn (September-November) — dark evenings from October mean late tests may be in darkness. Wet leaves on roads
  • Winter (December-February) — short days, rain, ice risk, and early darkness. However, if you can drive well in winter, you can drive well anytime

Practical Tips

  • Book your test as soon as your instructor says you are approaching test standard — waiting times can be 4-8 weeks
  • Check multiple test centres — one may have earlier availability than another
  • Cancellation slots appear regularly on the DVSA booking system — check daily for earlier dates
  • Book mid-week, mid-morning for the best conditions
  • Make sure your theory test certificate will still be valid on your chosen test date (2-year validity)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time of day for a driving test?

Mid-morning (10:00-11:00 AM) is generally considered ideal — rush hour is over and school-run traffic has cleared.

What day of the week is best?

Tuesday to Thursday. Traffic is predictable and consistent without the disruption of Monday mornings or Friday afternoon rush.

How far in advance should I book?

Book 4-8 weeks ahead when your instructor says you are approaching test standard. Check for cancellation slots daily to get an earlier date.
