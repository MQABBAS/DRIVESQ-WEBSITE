Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer driving lessons in Punjabi in Manchester?

Yes. DriveSQ has a fluent Punjabi-speaking driving instructor available across Greater Manchester. All instructions, explanations, and feedback can be delivered entirely in Punjabi or a mix of Punjabi and English — whatever helps you learn best.

How much do Punjabi speaking driving lessons cost?

Punjabi speaking lessons cost £33 per hour for both manual and automatic cars. There is no extra charge for instruction in Punjabi — the price is the same as our standard lessons.

Which areas in Manchester do you cover for Punjabi lessons?

We cover all of Greater Manchester including postcodes M1–M50, SK, BL, OL, and WN areas. This includes central Manchester, Stockport, Bolton, Oldham, Wigan, and all surrounding areas.

Can I take my driving test in Punjabi?

The DVSA driving test is conducted in English, but you can request a Punjabi interpreter through the DVSA at no extra cost. Your DriveSQ instructor will prepare you for all English commands used during the test so you feel confident on the day.

Do you offer intensive driving courses in Punjabi?

Yes. We offer intensive and semi-intensive driving courses with Punjabi-speaking instruction. These crash courses let you learn faster with multiple hours per day, all with instructions in Punjabi.

Is the theory test available in Punjabi?

The DVSA theory test is available in English only, but you can request a Punjabi voiceover or interpreter. Our free student learning app and your instructor will help you prepare for the theory test with Punjabi explanations of all key concepts.
