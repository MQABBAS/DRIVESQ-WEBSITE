Our Story

How DriveSQ Started

DriveSQ Driving School was founded in Manchester by instructors who saw a clear gap in the local market. Too many learner drivers across Greater Manchester were paying inflated prices for lessons that lacked structure, patience, and real test preparation. We set out to change that by building a driving school that prioritises three things above all else: affordability, quality instruction, and genuine care for every student who sits behind the wheel.

From our very first lesson, the principle has remained the same. We believe that learning to drive should not be a luxury reserved for those who can afford premium rates. Driving is a life skill, and access to professional, DVSA-approved instruction should be within reach of every learner in Manchester, Stockport, Bolton, Oldham, Wigan, and the surrounding areas. That is why we charge £33 per hour for both manual and automatic lessons — the same rate, no exceptions, no surge pricing, and no hidden booking fees.

What started as a small operation with a handful of dedicated instructors has grown into one of Manchester’s most trusted driving schools. Our growth has been entirely organic, built on word-of-mouth recommendations from students who passed their test and told their friends. We have never relied on flashy advertising or unrealistic promises. Instead, we let our results speak for themselves: a first-time pass rate that consistently exceeds 90 percent, a 4.9-star average across 87 verified reviews, and a reputation for patience that makes us the go-to school for nervous drivers and complete beginners alike.

Why We Do What We Do

Every member of the DriveSQ team became a driving instructor because they genuinely enjoy teaching. There is nothing quite like the moment a learner who once struggled with clutch control or mirror checks drives confidently through a busy roundabout for the first time. That transformation — from anxious beginner to competent, independent driver — is what motivates us every single day. We are not here to clock hours. We are here to help you pass your test and become a safe driver for life.

We also understand that learning to drive can be stressful. The pressure of the test, the fear of making mistakes, the financial commitment of weekly lessons — these are real concerns that many driving schools overlook. At DriveSQ, we address all of them. Our instructors are trained not only in DVSA-standard instruction but also in how to create a calm, supportive learning environment where mistakes are treated as opportunities to improve, not as failures. We work at your pace, not ours.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Everything we do at DriveSQ is guided by six principles that shape every lesson, every interaction, and every decision we make as a driving school. These are not marketing slogans — they are commitments that our instructors live by every day on the road.

Affordable Pricing for Everyone
We charge £33 per hour for every lesson, whether you are learning in a manual or automatic car. There are no booking fees, no admin charges, and no price increases mid-course. We believe that quality driving instruction should be accessible to all learners regardless of their budget. Many of our students are young people, university students, or working adults who need affordable rates without compromising on the quality of their education. We keep our overheads lean so we can pass the savings directly to you, making DriveSQ one of the most competitively priced DVSA-approved schools in the whole of Greater Manchester.
Patient, Supportive Instructors
Our instructors understand that everyone learns at a different pace and in a different way. Some students pick up clutch control in their first lesson while others need several sessions to feel comfortable. Both are completely normal, and our team never rushes, raises their voice, or makes you feel inadequate for needing more time. We specialise in working with nervous drivers and complete beginners who may have had negative experiences with other schools. Our calm, encouraging teaching style builds genuine confidence that stays with you long after you have passed your test and started driving independently on Manchester roads.
Structured Learning Programme
Every DriveSQ course follows a carefully designed curriculum that takes you from zero experience to test-ready in the most efficient way possible. We do not waste your time or money by letting you drive aimlessly around quiet estate roads for weeks on end. Each lesson has a specific objective, whether that is mastering junctions, perfecting parallel parking, or building confidence on dual carriageways. Your instructor tracks your progress using a detailed record system and adjusts the plan to focus on the areas where you need the most improvement, ensuring that every hour you spend with us is productive and moves you closer to your test date.
Test-Route Expertise
Our instructors know every test route used by DVSA examiners at Manchester test centres inside and out. This is not theoretical knowledge — it comes from years of accompanying students on tests and mapping every junction, roundabout, and potential hazard along the way. In the weeks before your test, we dedicate sessions to driving the exact routes you are likely to face, pointing out the spots where examiners pay closest attention and where previous candidates have made common mistakes. This targeted preparation gives you a significant advantage on test day and is one of the key reasons our first-time pass rate exceeds 90 percent consistently.
Flexible Scheduling
We know that your life does not revolve around driving lessons. You have work, university, family commitments, and social plans that make rigid scheduling impossible. That is why DriveSQ offers flexible lesson times seven days a week, including early mornings, evenings, and weekends. You can book lessons via WhatsApp with same-day confirmation, reschedule with reasonable notice, and choose between one-hour, ninety-minute, or two-hour sessions depending on what suits your availability and learning style. We also offer pick-up and drop-off from your home, workplace, or university campus anywhere within our coverage area.
Same Price: Manual & Automatic
Unlike many driving schools that charge a premium for automatic lessons, DriveSQ offers both manual and automatic instruction at the same £33 per hour rate. We believe that your choice of transmission should be based on what works best for you as a driver, not on what you can afford. Whether you want the full control of a manual gearbox or the simplicity of an automatic, the price is identical. Our automatic fleet is modern, well-maintained, and equipped with dual controls for your safety. Many of our students who struggle with clutch control in a manual switch to automatic and pass their test in significantly fewer lessons.

Meet the DriveSQ Team

Every instructor at DriveSQ is fully DVSA-approved, DBS-checked, and passionate about helping learners succeed. We do not hire on qualifications alone — we look for patience, communication skills, and a genuine desire to see students pass.

Meet Your Instructor
DVSA Approved
Every instructor holds a current DVSA Approved Driving Instructor badge, meaning they have passed all three ADI qualifying examinations and are registered with the Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency. This is your guarantee that you are learning from a qualified professional, not an unregistered trainer.
DBS Checked
All DriveSQ instructors have undergone enhanced Disclosure and Barring Service checks. Your safety is our absolute priority, and we maintain the highest standards of safeguarding for every student, regardless of age. Parents and guardians can book lessons with complete confidence.
Multilingual Support
Several of our instructors speak Urdu and Punjabi alongside English, making lessons more comfortable for students who prefer instruction in their first language. Clear communication is essential for safe, effective learning, and we ensure language is never a barrier to progress.
Nervous Driver Specialists
Our instructors have extensive experience working with anxious and nervous learners. They create a calm, pressure-free environment where you can make mistakes without fear of criticism. Many of our highest-rated reviews come from students who were terrified before their first lesson and passed with flying colours.

Why DriveSQ

Why Hundreds of Manchester Learners Choose Us

Choosing a driving school is one of the most important decisions you will make as a learner driver. The quality of your instructor, the structure of your lessons, and the value you get for your money will directly determine how quickly you progress, how confident you feel on test day, and whether you pass first time. At DriveSQ, we take that responsibility seriously.

Our students choose us for several reasons, but the most common feedback we receive centres on three things: our instructors are genuinely patient, our prices are honest and affordable, and our results are consistently excellent. We do not promise miracle pass rates or guarantee that you will be test-ready in a specific number of lessons, because every learner is different. What we do promise is that every lesson will be productive, every instructor will treat you with respect, and every pound you spend with us will move you closer to holding that pink licence.

Transparent Pricing With No Surprises

One of the biggest frustrations learner drivers face is hidden costs. Some schools advertise low introductory rates and then increase the price after the first few lessons. Others charge booking fees, cancellation penalties, or extra for weekend and evening slots. At DriveSQ, the price is £33 per hour, full stop. That applies to your first lesson and your fiftieth. It applies on Monday mornings and Saturday afternoons. It applies to manual and automatic cars equally. When you book with us, you know exactly what you are paying, and you will never receive an unexpected charge.

Real Test Preparation, Not Just Driving Practice

There is a significant difference between learning to drive and learning to pass the driving test. Both are important, and at DriveSQ we ensure you are prepared for both. In the early stages of your course, we focus on building core skills: steering control, gear changes, observations, and road positioning. As you progress, we introduce more complex manoeuvres, independent driving scenarios, and hazard perception techniques. In the final phase before your test, we shift to intensive test-route practice on the exact roads used by DVSA examiners, combined with mock test sessions that replicate the pressure and format of the real examination.

A Community-Driven Driving School

DriveSQ is not a franchise or a corporate chain. We are a local Manchester business, built by local instructors, serving local communities. We understand the roads, the traffic patterns, the school-run bottlenecks, and the quirks of Manchester driving that national chains simply cannot replicate. When you learn with DriveSQ, you are supporting a small business that reinvests in the community by keeping prices low, hiring local instructors, and providing a service that genuinely helps people gain independence and opportunity through driving.
