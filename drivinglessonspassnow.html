Our Blueprint

The DriveSQ Pass-Ready Framework

We replace anxiety with reliable mechanical logic. Our core operational pillars ensure you stay in complete control, even on peak-hour test runs.

01

The 5-Second Mirror Loop

An unhurried operational loop executed before modifying speed or tracking: Rear-View (speed/distance calculation) → Left-Mirror (cyclist protection) → Right-Mirror (overtaking identification) → Forward Hazards scan → Decisive, timed implementation.

02

Manchester Gap-Check Sequence

Eliminates dangerous creeping behaviors at hectic junctions. Follow our strict protocol: Hold Firm (full line balance) → Scan & Target (fixate clear reference signposts) → Commit & Clear (clean speed execution without mid-turn braking checks).

03

Centre-Choice Compass

Eliminate test-day variables. We map out arrival patterns for your chosen centre, pacing parameters exactly 20 minutes early, handbrake securing procedures, and early satellite-navigation cut-offs to quieten focus levels completely.
