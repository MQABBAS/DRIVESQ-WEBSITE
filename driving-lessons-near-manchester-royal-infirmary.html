Driving Lessons Near the MRI

Manchester Royal Infirmary on Oxford Road is one of the city's largest employers, with thousands of staff, students, and visitors passing through daily. For NHS workers on rotating shifts, finding a driving school that accommodates irregular hours can be challenging. DriveSQ offers fully flexible scheduling—including early mornings, late evenings, and weekends—making it possible to learn to drive around even the most unpredictable work patterns.

We provide convenient pickup from safe locations near the MRI, including side streets off Oxford Road, the university campus area, and residential streets in Rusholme and Ardwick. Your instructor works around your availability, not the other way around.

Driving on Oxford Road

Oxford Road is one of Manchester's most complex driving environments: bus lanes, cycle lanes, pedestrian crossings, university traffic, and emergency vehicles create a demanding but incredibly educational setting. DriveSQ uses the Oxford Road corridor as an advanced-skills module, teaching you to manage multi-user road spaces safely and confidently. This experience makes you a stronger driver on any UK road.

Scheduling Around Shifts

Hospital shift patterns do not follow a nine-to-five schedule, and neither do we. DriveSQ instructors near the MRI offer lesson slots from 7 AM through to 8 PM, seven days a week. Whether you are coming off a night shift, have a gap between day shifts, or are free on a random Wednesday afternoon, we will find a slot that works. Our WhatsApp booking system makes rescheduling quick and painless when shifts change at short notice.

Building Confidence for Essential Commuting

Many MRI staff learn to drive specifically to improve their commute—public transport timetables do not always align with hospital shift times, and driving offers reliability that buses and trams cannot match. DriveSQ understands this practical motivation and structures your lessons to get you driving independently as efficiently as possible, without cutting corners on safety or skills.
