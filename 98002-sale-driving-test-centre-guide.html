Sale Driving Test Centre — Complete Guide

Test Centre Details

Sale Driving Test Centre is on Marsland Road, Sale M33 3AQ. It covers Sale, Stretford, Urmston, Altrincham, Timperley, and parts of Trafford. The centre has a small car park and is close to Sale town centre.

Current Pass Rate

Sale has a pass rate of approximately 46-49%, close to the national average. Routes combine suburban streets, dual carriageways, and residential areas with varying speed limits. DriveSQ learners consistently pass above 88% at Sale.

Common Test Routes

Typical routes use Marsland Road, Washway Road (A56), Northenden Road, and Cross Street. Expect dual carriageway driving on the A56 towards Altrincham or Stretford, residential areas around Brooklands with speed humps, and the Sale town centre one-way system. Some routes include approaches to the M60 junction but you will not drive on the motorway itself.

Difficult Junctions and Hazards

  • A56 Washway Road — fast-moving traffic requires confident lane changes
  • Sale town centre one-way system — multiple lanes with short distances between turns
  • Marsland Road / Washway Road junction — busy traffic-light junction
  • Brooklands Road speed humps — maintain appropriate speed without harsh braking
  • Cross Street / Northenden Road roundabout — multi-exit roundabout requiring lane discipline
  • School zones in Brooklands and Ashton-upon-Mersey — 20mph limits

Tips from DriveSQ Instructors

  • Master the A56 lane discipline — this road features on almost every route
  • Practise the Sale one-way system until it feels automatic
  • Speed humps in Brooklands catch learners who brake too late — ease off early
  • Watch your speed on residential streets — many have 20mph limits
  • The car park is tight — arrive with your instructor to avoid parking stress
  • Stay calm at the busy Washway Road junctions — patience is key

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the pass rate at Sale?

Approximately 46-49%. DriveSQ learners regularly pass at 88%+ through thorough route preparation.

What roads does the Sale test cover?

Expect the A56 Washway Road, Marsland Road, Brooklands residential streets, and Sale town centre one-way systems.

Is there parking at Sale test centre?

There is a small car park. Street parking on Marsland Road and nearby streets is usually available if you arrive early.

Sale Local Area Guide

Lessons around Sale use real local roads including Marsland Road, Northenden Road and School Road, so by the time you're ready for your test you've already driven the streets you'll use every day after passing. Sale sits on the line of the old Roman road linking the forts at Chester and Manchester, and the modern A56 through the town still follows that ancient route.

We also plan around school-run traffic near Park Road Sale Primary School and Firs Primary School, using quieter spots like Sale Town Hall for early manoeuvre practice before stepping up to busier sections of Marsland Road.
