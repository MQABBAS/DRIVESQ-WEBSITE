Your Structured Lesson Plan

Every hour is planned with purpose. Here is a typical breakdown of how your 10 hours are structured with DriveSQ.

Hours 1-2

Assessment & Foundations

Your instructor evaluates your current ability on a familiar route. Together you identify strengths and areas needing attention. You will recap essential observations, mirror checks, and road positioning.

Hours 3-4

Junctions, Roundabouts & Emerging

Deep-dive into complex junction work including T-junctions, crossroads, and multi-lane roundabouts. Focus on timing, observation, and positioning under various traffic conditions.

Hours 5-6

Manoeuvres & Dual Carriageways

Master all four test manoeuvres: parallel park, forward bay park, reverse bay park, and pulling up on the right. Then progress to dual carriageway driving for higher-speed confidence.

Hours 7-8

Independent Driving & Test Routes

Practise navigating independently using sat-nav and road signs. Drive popular test routes from your local test centre so you are familiar with the roads and common hazards on test day.

Hours 9-10

Mock Tests & Final Refinement

Complete one or two full mock tests under exam conditions. Your instructor provides detailed feedback, helping you correct any remaining faults so you walk into your test feeling fully prepared.
