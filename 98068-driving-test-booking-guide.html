How to Book Your Driving Test — Step by Step

Before You Book

  • You must hold a valid UK provisional driving licence
  • You must have passed your theory test (your certificate must be valid — it expires after 2 years)
  • Your instructor must confirm you are approaching test standard
  • Decide which test centre you want — discuss with your DriveSQ instructor

Booking Online

Book at GOV.UK — this is the only official booking site. You need your provisional driving licence number, your theory test pass certificate number, and a debit or credit card. The test costs £62 for weekday tests and £75 for evening and weekend tests. Choose your preferred test centre, select an available date and time, and pay online. You will receive a booking confirmation by email.

Getting an Earlier Date

  • Check the booking system daily — cancellations create new slots regularly
  • Try different test centres — one may have earlier availability than your first choice
  • Avoid booking during peak periods (January-March and September-November when universities restart)
  • Some third-party apps alert you to cancellation slots — use cautiously and only official services
  • Your DriveSQ instructor may know which centres typically have shorter waiting times

Changing or Cancelling Your Test

You can change your test date or centre online with at least 3 clear working days notice at no charge. Cancel with 3 clear working days notice for a full refund. Less than 3 working days notice and you lose the full fee. If you are not ready, it is better to postpone than to take the test unprepared and fail.

Test Day Requirements

Bring your provisional driving licence (photocard). Arrive at least 10 minutes before your appointment. Your DriveSQ instructor will provide the car. If you arrive late, the test is cancelled and you lose your fee. If your licence is expired, the test cannot proceed.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does the driving test cost?

£62 for weekday tests, £75 for evening and weekend tests. Book online at GOV.UK.

How long is the wait for a driving test?

Typically 4-8 weeks depending on the centre and time of year. Check daily for cancellation slots to get an earlier date.

Can I book a test before I am ready?

Yes, but we recommend booking when your instructor says you are approaching test standard. You can always postpone with 3 days notice.
