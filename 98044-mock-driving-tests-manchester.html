Mock Driving Tests in Manchester

What Is a Mock Driving Test?

A mock test is a full simulation of the real driving test, conducted by your DriveSQ instructor using examiner-standard marking criteria. It covers the eyesight check, show-me-tell-me questions, general driving, independent driving, one manoeuvre, and possibly an emergency stop — exactly mirroring what happens on test day. The key difference is that your instructor gives detailed feedback on every fault, helping you fix issues before they cost you a real test.

Why Mock Tests Work

  • They reveal faults you did not know you were making — many are habitual and invisible to you
  • They simulate test pressure so the real day feels familiar, not terrifying
  • They use actual examiner routes from your chosen test centre
  • They provide measurable benchmarks — you can track your improvement across multiple mocks
  • They highlight the difference between a minor fault and a serious fault, so you know what to prioritise

How We Run Mock Tests

Your instructor drives you to the test centre area, then acts as examiner — giving only directions, not teaching input. You drive for 40 minutes exactly as you would on the real test. Your instructor marks faults on a standard marking sheet. Afterwards, you review every fault together, discuss why each one occurred, and create an action plan. Most learners need 3-5 mock tests before their real test.

Mock Test Pricing

A standalone mock test costs £35 and takes approximately 90 minutes including the debrief. Alternatively, mock tests are included in our intensive course packages and test preparation bundles. Many learners combine a mock test with a standard lesson — one hour of focused practice followed by a full mock.

When to Start Mock Tests

Begin mock tests when your instructor believes you are approaching test standard — typically when you can drive independently with minimal instructor input. Starting too early wastes time; starting too late means less time to correct faults. Your DriveSQ instructor will tell you when the time is right.
