Failsworth Driving Test Centre — Complete Guide

Failsworth tests your ability to handle mixed environments: fast A-roads, industrial junctions, residential streets, and complex roundabouts. DriveSQ instructors know every route and every fail point.

Centre Information

Failsworth Test Centre serves East Manchester and parts of Oldham. Pass rate: approximately 42-46%. Routes feature the A62 Oldham Road, Broadway roundabout, Hollinwood interchange, and a mix of residential and industrial roads.

Critical Route Challenges

A62 Oldham Road Speed Management

The A62 transitions between 30mph and 40mph zones with limited signage. Missing a speed limit change is an automatic serious fault. DriveSQ teaches active sign scanning — checking lamp posts for repeater signs as a continuous habit, not a one-off check.

Broadway Roundabout

A multi-lane roundabout that feeds traffic between Failsworth, Oldham, and the M60 motorway. Correct lane selection is critical. DriveSQ approaches this roundabout from all four entry points during training, ensuring you can handle any direction the examiner requests.

Hollinwood Junction Interchange

This complex junction connects local roads with the M60 motorway system. Multiple lanes, high-speed merges, and rapid lane changes test your confidence and observation. DriveSQ builds up to this junction gradually — starting with simpler roundabouts and progressing as your multi-lane skills develop.

Industrial Estate Junctions

Failsworth routes pass through industrial areas with HGV traffic, wide access roads, and unusual junction layouts. Large vehicles restrict your view, requiring extra caution at junctions. DriveSQ teaches the "lean and look" technique — adjusting your position at junctions to see past obstructing vehicles.
