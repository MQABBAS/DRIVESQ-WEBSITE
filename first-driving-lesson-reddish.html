Driving Lessons in Reddish

Why Learn to Drive in Reddish?

Reddish is a compact residential area in Stockport with a strong industrial heritage, centred around the former Houldsworth Mill complex. The area blends Victorian terraces with newer housing and benefits from good rail links via Reddish North and Reddish South stations. Reddish Vale Country Park provides a green escape along the River Tame.

Driving Challenges in Reddish

Reddish's Victorian street layout means many narrow roads with on-street parking reducing the carriageway to single-lane. The approaches to Houldsworth Village involve tight turns and hidden driveways. Gorton Road carries heavy traffic between Stockport and Gorton, demanding confident lane discipline at busy junctions.

Our Top Tips for Reddish Learners

Practise your parallel parking on the terraced streets around Houldsworth Street where spaces are tight but plentiful. Use the quieter roads around Reddish Vale for building confidence before tackling Gorton Road. Be patient at the Broadstone Road roundabout during school run times as queues build quickly.

Test Centre: Your nearest test centre is Bredbury, approximately 2.1 miles from Reddish. Your DriveSQ instructor provides the car on test day at no extra charge.

Transport & Parking

Reddish North station provides services to Manchester Piccadilly and Rose Hill Marple, while Reddish South connects to Stockport and Stalybridge. Several bus routes run along Gorton Road and Reddish Road.

On-street parking dominates the terraced streets and can be very tight. The small car parks near the shops on Gorton Road fill up quickly during the day.
