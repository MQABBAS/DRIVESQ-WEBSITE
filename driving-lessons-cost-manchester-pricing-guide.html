Manchester Average Prices

How Many Lessons Will I Need?

The DVSA recommends 45 hours of professional tuition plus 22 hours of private practice. In reality:


  • Complete beginner: 35-50 hours (automatic) or 40-55 hours (manual)

  • Some experience: 15-30 hours

  • Refresher: 5-10 hours

  • Intensive course: 20-40 hours in 1-2 weeks


Total cost for a complete beginner: approximately £1,100 to £1,750 for lessons, plus £23 (theory) and £62 (practical test) = roughly £1,200 to £1,850 all-in.
